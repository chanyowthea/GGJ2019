# GGJ2019

## Intro

When you wake up, you find that you lost one of your stocking, and most of the rooms in your house turn upside-down, but fortunately, you gain an ability to reverse your room. Tide and recover your room to the normal order, and find your lost stocking. instructions: W A S D to move J K L I U O to reverse the current room. And have a good time!
